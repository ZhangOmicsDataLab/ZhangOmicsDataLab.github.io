Hector Mauricio Castaneda Cortes (Researcher)

Hector is an accomplished researcher with expertise in bioinformatics and computational biology. He has a strong background in developing and applying computational models to analyze high-throughput biological data. His research focuses on transcriptomics, proteomics, and the development of tools for analyzing complex RNA-seq datasets. Hector has contributed to several research projects that enhance our understanding of gene expression and protein function in various biological systems.

He has worked with numerous international research teams and has gained significant experience in integrating computational techniques with experimental biology, particularly in high-throughput data analysis.
