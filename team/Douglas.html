Douglas Freeburn

Douglas is a PhD student in the omicsDatalab working in transcriptomics. He is the recipient of an MSci in Bioinformatics, awarded by the University of Dundee for his work in transcriptional control of thermomorphogenesis. Currently, Douglas is working towards his PhD, using both Python and R to develop novel computational tools for the analysis of Oxford Nanopore Sequencing data, and is expected to graduate in 2027.
